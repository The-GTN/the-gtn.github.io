Human Computer Interactions
---

It was an introduction lecture about Human Computer Interactions. In this course, we introduce 3nd CS bachelor students of University of Lille to user-centered design of systems with questionnaires, semi-structured interviews, brainstorming and sketching. We also introduce to implement some interfaces, using PyQt python library.

For more information : <a target="_blank" href="https://portail.fil.univ-lille.fr/portail/index.php?dipl=L&sem=S5&ue=IHM&label=Pr%C3%A9sentation">https://portail.fil.univ-lille.fr/portail/index.php?dipl=L&sem=S5&ue=IHM&label=Pr%C3%A9sentation</a>
